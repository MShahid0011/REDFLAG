Field notes — what to watch for

  • Urgency & threats. "Act now or lose access" is designed to short-circuit careful thinking.
  • Mismatched domains. Check the actual sender address and link destination, not just the display name.
  • Requests for secrecy. Legitimate requests rarely ask you to bypass normal verification or tell no one.
  • Unsolicited contact. Real institutions rarely cold-call or text you demanding immediate sensitive action.
  • Too good to be true. Unusually high pay, prizes, or refunds are common lures.
