What you'll practice

Four ways attackers reach you

📧

Phishing emails

Spoofed domains, urgency, fake invoices, and credential-harvesting links disguised as routine messages.

💬

Smishing (SMS)

Fake delivery alerts, bank warnings, and account security texts designed to trigger a fast, unverified click.

☎

Vishing (calls)

Tech-support scams and impersonation calls that pressure you into granting access or revealing information.

🏢

Workplace fraud

CEO fraud, invoice fraud, and fake job offers that exploit trust, hierarchy, and routine business processes.
